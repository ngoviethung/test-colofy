Providing steel wide ranges of power industry projects, how big is Thanh Long Group Joint Stock Company?
13rdAug2023
Under the leadership of group president Vũ Thành Long (born in 1967), Thanh Long Corporation Joint Stock Company has won sever al contracts to supply steel poles for electrical projects, achieving revenue exceeding billions of Vietnamese đồng in 2019.
Van Phong Thermal Power Plant

In July 2021, Thành Long Corporation Joint Stock Company (Thành Long) had its contract terminated by the Central Power Corporation of Central Vietnam (EVNCPC) for the CPC-DX.HA-G03 Package Contract, which involved supplying steel columns for the 110kV Duy Xuyen - Hoi An Transmission Line Project. According to EVNCPC, the reason for termination was Thành Long's contract violations and delays in project implementation.

 

However, this incident seems to have had no impact on Thành Long's bidding activities. Recently, the company won two major contracts simultaneously related to the supply and transportation of steel columns for the 500kV Van Phong Thermal Power Plant - Vinh Tan Thermal Power Plant Transmission Line Project.

These contracts are part of Package Contract No. 12 for the Supply and Transportation of Steel Columns. Specifically, Lot 12.6 involves supplying and transporting steel columns from G27A to G32 (including G32) with a winning bid price of 113.877 billion VND, while Lot 12.7 involves supplying and transporting steel columns from G27A to G32 (including G32) with a winning bid price of 93.314 billion VND.

Van Phong Thermal Power Plant

Data from VietTimes shows that from 2019 until now, Thành Long has also won several other notable contracts. These include the SPC-KfW3-G4 Package Contract for supplying steel poles for Component 3 - Efficient Power Grids in medium and small cities in the southern region, funded by the German Government's Official Development Assistance (Component 3 - Phase 1) - 7 sub-projects at 110kV, with a winning bid price of 138 billion VND (contract price: 141.7 billion VND); Package Contract No. 14 for the Supply and Transportation of Steel Columns (from G10-1 to the endpoint) for the Lao Bao 220kV Substation and the Dong Ha - Lao Bao 220kV Transmission Line Project, with a winning bid price of 43.6 billion VND (contract price: 70.5 billion VND); and Package Contract No. 13 for the Supply and Transportation of Steel Columns for the section from position T15 to DC (including both columns and steel cross-arms within the switchyard) of the Long Thanh 500kV Substation Project, with a winning bid price of 68.7 billion VND (contract price: 74.5 billion VND).

 

In February 2021, the consortium of Thành Long Corporation, Viet Hung Construction and Technical Services Joint Stock Company (Viet Hung), and CIENCO1 won Package Contract No. 9 for the Construction of Bridges, Barriers, Lines, Lighting, and Signals for the Overpass Construction Project at the intersection of Chua Boc - Pham Ngoc Thach, Dong Da District, with a winning bid price of 102.7 billion VND (contract price: 120.1 billion VND).the Board of Directors and the legal representative is Mr. Vu Thanh Long (born in 1967). In July 2015, Thành Long tripled its charter capital to 980 billion VND.
