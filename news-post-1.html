Thanh Long Group Joint Stock Company celebrates National Day (2/9)
13rdAug2023
On the occasion of the 71st anniversary of the August Revolution and National Day (September 2nd), Thành Long Corporation Joint Stock Company held a banquet to express gratitude and honor the foreign advisors, experts, strategic partners, as well as all the staff, engineers, and employees
on this day
Thành Long Corporation Joint Stock Company specializes in manufacturing and processing steel structures used in industrial construction, fabricating structural products, steel beams, workshop frames, bridges, and national thermal power projects. They also undertake national projects such as 500kV high-voltage power transmission towers and steel overpasses. Additionally, they produce galvanized steel structures. The factory has a production capacity of 1,000 tons per month.
2/9

Currently, Thành Long's distinct mark can be found in various transportation and national power transmission projects spanning from the North to the South, showcasing their significant contribution to the construction industry. Moreover, Thành Long Corporation also exports several projects to the international market, including Laos, South Korea, Thailand, Pakistan, among others. Their PTC products, such as the NDTB-PACO project, have been highly regarded by international clients for product quality and construction progress.

Thành Long Corporation has successfully collaborated with major Japanese economic corporations such as KaWADA and TOA on multiple projects. With annual revenue reaching billions of Vietnamese dong, the corporation provides employment for over 800 staff members.

The company's success is attributed to the right mindset and direction of the leadership team, as well as the dedication and unity of its employees, engineers, and workers who work tirelessly day and nigh
