Thanh Long Joint Stock Company signed a contract with Islamabad Power Company of Pakistan
13rdAug2023
On January 10, 2018, in the capital city Islamabad (Pakistan), with the support of the Vietnamese Embassy in Pakistan, Thành Long Joint Stock Company, led by Mr. Vũ Thành Long - Chairman of the Board of Directors, signed a contract with Islamabad Power Company to supply steel electric poles with a total value of 2.5 million dollars. This contract was signed within the framework of the government of Pakistan's power grid development project, funded by the Asian Development Bank (ADB), and marks the second contract that Thành Long Group has signed with a Pakistani partner in the past three years
an important contract marked as a huge and fast develoment of the group
Thành Long Corporation Joint Stock Company specializes in manufacturing and processing steel structures used in industrial construction, fabricating structural products, steel beams, workshop frames, bridges, and national thermal power projects. They also undertake national projects such as 500kV high-voltage power transmission towers and steel overpasses. Additionally, they produce galvanized steel structures. The factory has a production capacity of 1,000 tons per month.

To secure this valuable contract, Thành Long Corporation had to prove itself as a capable contractor with four manufacturing plants, producing approximately 3,000 tons of finished steel per month. They are currently the supplier for around 70% of the steel power transmission towers in Vietnam. They ensured the successful execution of the steel power transmission tower supply contract to Pakistan, with a total volume of approximately 2,500 tons over a period of six months, including the time for design, installation, and acceptance.

This contract represents a specific step towards implementing the outcomes of the 4th meeting of the Vietnam-Pakistan Joint Government Committee and Trade Sub-Committee, held in Islamabad in October 2017. It encourages businesses from both countries to enhance cooperation, thereby promoting bilateral trade between Vietnam and Pakistan.
