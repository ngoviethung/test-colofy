recruiting Regulatory Affairs staff - THANH LONG GROUP
13rdAug2023

JOB DESCRIPTION

  • Responsible for legal and regulatory issues of the company.
  • Participate in drafting contracts and company documents, ensuring their legality and compliance with the law for company transactions.
  • Advise and consult the Board of Directors on legal matters.
  • Research and stay updated on changes in laws, regulations, and related activities in the company's business operations.
  • Check the system of policies and ensure that the company's policies are in compliance with relevant legal regulations.
  • Manage legal documents and records, handle legal issues arising in the company.
  • Perform other tasks as directed by superiors.

JOB REQUIREMENTS

  • University degree in Law.
  • Experience in a similar position is preferred.
  • Strong problem-solving and situational handling skills.
  • Good communication, confidence, and flexibility in work.
  • Proficient in office computer skills.

JOB BENEFITS

  • Dynamic, modern, and professional working environment.
  • Competitive salary and bonuses based on performance and job effectiveness.
  • Opportunities for professional and skill development training.
  • Statutory social insurance and health insurance benefits.
  • Other welfare benefits as regulated by the company.
  • Company-provided accommodation for employees.
  • Transportation services available.
